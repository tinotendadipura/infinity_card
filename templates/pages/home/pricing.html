FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and plans

How does the NFC card + subscription work?
You purchase a physical NFC card once (one-time fee). Once it's delivered, you pick a monthly or yearly hosting plan that powers your digital profile. Tap your card on any phone to share your profile instantly.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes! You can upgrade instantly at any time and we'll prorate the cost. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle, so you keep your features until then.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept PayPal, bank transfer, and EcoCash. All payments are processed securely and you'll receive a confirmation once approved.
Is there a free trial?
Your subscription starts only after your NFC card is delivered. There's no charge until you choose a plan, and you can start with our affordable Starter plan at just $3/month.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard. Your profile will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
What happens if my subscription expires?
If your subscription expires, your public profile will be temporarily suspended. Your data is kept safe and your profile will be restored as soon as you renew.
