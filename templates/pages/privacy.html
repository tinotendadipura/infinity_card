Privacy Policy How we protect your data

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your username, email address, and password. When you build your profile, you may provide additional information such as your name, phone number, location, bio, profile image, and social media links.

When visitors tap your NFC card, we collect basic analytics data including IP address, browser user agent, and timestamp to provide you with tap statistics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account and profile
  • To display your public profile to visitors
  • To provide tap analytics on your dashboard
  • To manage your subscription and billing
  • To respond to support requests and contact form submissions
  • To improve our platform and services

3. Information Sharing

Your public profile information is visible to anyone who visits your profile URL or taps your NFC card. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

We may share information with service providers who assist in operating our platform (hosting, payment processing) under strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure password hashing (PBKDF2), CSRF protection, and XSS prevention headers. Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Cookies

We use session cookies for authentication and CSRF protection. These are essential cookies required for the platform to function and cannot be opted out of.

6. Your Rights

  • Access and view your personal data through your dashboard
  • Edit or update your information at any time
  • Delete your account by contacting support
  • Unpublish your profile to hide it from public view

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@inftycard.cc or use our contact form.
